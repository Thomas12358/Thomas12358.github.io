# lifeOS: Personal Life Operating System

A collection of Google Apps Scripts designed to create a unified personal management system that integrates with Google Workspace.

## Overview

lifeOS provides powerful automation and organization tools built with Google Apps Script, enabling seamless integration across Google Sheets, Docs, Calendar, and Gmail.

## Features

- **Automated Workflows**: Create custom workflows tailored to your personal or professional needs
- **Data Integration**: Connect and synchronize data across multiple Google services
- **Task Management**: Organize and automate your task tracking system
- **Reporting**: Generate automated reports and analytics

## Implementation Details

The lifeOS system leverages:

- **Google Apps Script**: Serverless scripting platform for Google Workspace
- **Google Sheets**: Data storage and management
- **Google Calendar**: Event automation and scheduling
- **Gmail**: Email automation and management
